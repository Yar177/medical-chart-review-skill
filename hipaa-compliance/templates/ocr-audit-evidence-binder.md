## 9. Audit-window response readiness

When an audit notice arrives:

- [ ] Acknowledge promptly (within OCR's requested window)
- [ ] Engage counsel + privacy officer + security officer immediately
- [ ] Issue litigation/audit hold to relevant systems and personnel
- [ ] Designate single point of contact for all OCR communications
- [ ] Use this binder to compile responses
- [ ] Track every request, every response, every decision
- [ ] Communications about the audit subject to legal hold

## Retention

All items retained ≥ 6 years per 164.530(j) (Privacy Rule documentation standard) plus any longer period required by state law or litigation hold.
