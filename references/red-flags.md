# Clinical Red Flags — Must-Not-Miss

Surface as **Critical findings** with citation when any of the following appear in the record:

## Patient safety

- Documented allergy with subsequent order/administration of the allergen (or cross-reactant)
- Critical lab value (see `labs-imaging.md`) without follow-up note acknowledging action
- High-risk medication combinations (warfarin + interacting drug; opioid + benzo; serotonergic combos)
- Wrong-patient / wrong-site / wrong-procedure documentation
- Fall risk + restraint without proper documentation
- Pressure injury stage worsening without intervention escalation
- DVT prophylaxis omitted in inpatient without documented contraindication
- Sepsis criteria met (e.g., 2+ SIRS + suspected infection, or qSOFA ≥2, or SOFA increase ≥2) without sepsis bundle (lactate, cultures, abx within 1–3 hr, fluids)
- STEMI/NSTEMI features without timely ECG/troponin/cards consult
- Stroke symptoms without NIHSS, imaging, tPA/thrombectomy evaluation
- Anaphylaxis without epinephrine

## Mental health & safety

- Suicidal/homicidal ideation without:
  - Safety assessment
  - Means restriction discussion
  - Disposition decision (admit vs safety plan)
  - Lethal means counseling for firearms access
- Domestic violence / abuse disclosure without resources / safety planning / mandatory reporting where applicable
- Child abuse / elder abuse / vulnerable adult signs — mandatory reporting

## OB/peds

- Pregnancy + Category D/X medication
- Decreased fetal movement, vaginal bleeding, severe HTN in pregnancy without urgent eval
- Newborn jaundice without bilirubin level / nomogram
- Failure-to-thrive without workup
- Immunizations significantly behind schedule

## Chronic disease management gaps

- Diabetic without A1c in past year
- HTN without recent BP / med titration despite uncontrolled readings
- HF with EF <40% without GDMT (ACEi/ARB/ARNI, BB, MRA, SGLT2i) or contraindication
- Atrial fibrillation with CHA₂DS₂-VASc ≥2 without anticoagulation or documented reason
- Post-MI without statin / antiplatelet
- COPD with frequent exacerbations without inhaler optimization or pulmonary rehab

## Documentation/legal red flags

- Procedure performed without documented informed consent
- Restraint use without order, justification, and reassessment
- Code status change without conversation documentation
- Death without time/cause/notification documentation
- Significant adverse event (fall with injury, med error, never event) without incident report reference
- Telemedicine encounter without consent/location/modality documentation

## Care transitions

- Discharge from inpatient/ED for high-acuity condition without:
  - Follow-up appointment
  - Medication list with reconciliation
  - Red-flag return precautions
  - Communication to PCP
- Hospital readmission within 30 days without root-cause analysis trail

## Coding/billing red flags

- Diagnosis billed but not in documentation
- Cloned A/P across multiple dates
- Time-based E&M without time documented
- Procedure CPT without procedure note
- HCC dropped from prior year with no resolution documented (may be valid or may be missed capture)

## How to report

```
🔴 CRITICAL — [Category]
Finding: [one-line summary]
Evidence: [chart citation with date + note type + section]
Why this matters: [clinical/regulatory rationale]
Suggested action: [escalate to attending / query provider / add to safety report]
```
